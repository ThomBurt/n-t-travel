Bosnia & Herzegovina

This is a country with influence from the East and the West with many small towns having Ottoman influence, including the beautiful bridge in Mostar.
When the Ottomans invaded hundreds of years ago they bought Islam with them, so there is more than 1 main religion here and it is mirrored in the architecture. Truly special.
