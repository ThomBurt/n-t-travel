What documentation should I travel with?
If you are staying within your country will just need some form of ID, and a driving license if you rent a car. For international travel you will need a passport and another form of ID (driving license if you hire a car) so you can leave your passport in a safe space.
How much should I budget for spending money?
This will completely depend on your trip. We have been on trips with an unbelievably low budget of no more than $10 a day and also a much higher budget. If you want to enjoy restaurants then I would look to save for at least $100 a day per person to fully enjoy your trip!
