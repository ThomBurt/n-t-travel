Packing List

What to keep on you

Some items are important to keep on your person. For flights Thom often wears pants with zips on the pockets to keep his phone, wallet and passport safe.

What to keep in your pockets


  • Passport
  • Phone
  • Wallet
  • Tickets

What to pack in your carry on

A huge travel hack is to use your carry on bag to your advantage for extra room.
It is always important to have at least one change of clothes with you, this is a precaution in case your suitcase gets lost.
All of your smaller electronics and other important items should also go in your carry on. Get a good quality carry on bag with pockets and a good zip.

What to pack in your carry on bag


  • Spare clothes
  • Laptop / Tablet
  • Charging cables and plug adapters
  • Headphones
  • Eye Mask
  • Camera
  • Hat
  • Sunglasses

What to pack in your suitcase

A good rule-of-thumb is to save the majority of your suitcase for clothes, toiletries and any souvenirs/gifts you wish to bring back home with you

We will only travel with a suitcase for any trip over a few days, if it is a quick 1-4 day trip you can utilize your carry on bag if you purchase a decent one with plenty of room.

What to pack in your suitcase


  • Clothes
  • Toiletries
  • Spare cables
  • Souvenirs/Gifts
  • Any other miscellaneous items
