Split, Croatia

Croatia might have gone under the radar until now, but trust me! You want to visit here, and Split is a great starting point. A large chunk of 'Game of Thrones' was filmed here, and when you're walking around Diocletian's Palace it is not hard to see why!

Day One: Exploring Split

Arriving at Split airport, you will exit to find the taxi rank and take a 30 minute ride into old town Split, otherwise known as 'Diocletian's Palace'.
What you will soon discover is the entire old town was built inside 'Diocletian's Palace' in the 4th century AD.

Accommodation in Split...

The best accommodation 'Split' has to offer.

While walking around the charming old town, you are met with shops, bars, restaurants and an entire harbor and ocean front.
We recommend taking the 20 minute stroll from the old town up to the Prva vidilica na Marjanu look out point where you can see the whole city including 'Diocletian's Palace', the harbor to way in the distance the 'Fortress of Klis' which is what they used to film 'Mereen' in 'Game of Thrones'.

Speaking of 'Game of Thrones' be sure not to miss the museum, as you will get the opportunity to see behind the scenes photos of the filming period.

Day Two: Krka National Park

Krka National Park is situated along the Krka River in southern Croatia. It's known for a series of 7 waterfalls.
In the south, Skradinski Buk waterfall is flanked by traditional watermills. To the north, a nature trail passes another striking cascade, Roški Slap, and the Krka Monastery, built above ancient Roman catacombs. Visovac Island is home to the 15th-century Franciscan Monastery of Our Lady of Mercy.

We recommend taking a day trip tour from Split, they make it really easy and you will get transport to the national park, swimming, food & wine tasting along the way.

Get your tickets ahead of time: Krka Waterfalls, Swimming, Food & Wine Tasting

Day Three: 5 Islands Day Trip including Hvar and Blue Lagoon

Head on an adventurous small-group or private 5-island tour by speedboat, including snorkeling in pristine blue waters, a visit to the charming village of Komiža, and sightseeing in Hvar.

Get your tickets ahead of time: Blue Cave, Hvar, Blue Lagoon, Komiža, Stivina Cove Day Trip
