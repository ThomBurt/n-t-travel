Nice to meet you!

We’re cheaper than a travel agency and ready to match both your budget and dreams. Niki finds the coolest views, restaurants, and streets to wander, while Thom specializes in logistics such as transportation, routes, and rentals.

We’re a husband-and-wife team and met in 2014 while staying at the same hostel in Cairns, Australia. Thom is from London and Niki, from Indiana. We currently live in the USA and have lived in England, Australia, New Zealand, and Spain.

We started this business after planning and going on hundreds of our own dream trips. We’ve loved each place we’ve visited and accredit the smooth travels to the hours of research we pour into making each experience incredible before we even arrive.

We love to leave room for flexibility on trips (that’s the spirit behind it right?) but if you’ve ever been that person visiting the Louvre, only to find out that it’s booked for the next month, you know that a bit of research, planning, and booking in advance will make your trip as perfect as possible.

After seeing many of our friends turn to travel agencies who charged them way too much for simple tasks, we set out to help people plan AMAZING trips on a reasonable budget. Oh, and we don't receive commission based on our suggestions, so you can trust that our itineraries are unbiased and custom-built for you.

What do we do?



Any Country


We’re ready to make a day-by-day itinerary for any location you’d like to visit. We will also include a summary of the culture, a packing list and top tips for traveling.

Our 20 years of combined travel research experience has taught us exactly how to find the best of the best - no matter the destination.

We know exactly what to look out for and what sites to trust.



Any Budget


All budgets are welcome here! Whether you’re limited to a few dollars a day or want a luxury experience, we’ll uncover the best options in your price range.

It’s important for us to find the best value for your money, no matter how much you have to spend.

We don’t do commission, so you can trust that every option we list is unbiased and authentic.



Best Flights


We research the best flights, for the best prices, with the least layovers.

We use industry hacks to do this, such as researching the recommended cheapest booking windows and using flight comparison tools.

We’ll give you options and always keep an eye out for a cool layover that could enhance your trip.



Top Accommodations


Trust us, you want a comfortable, clean, and trustworthy spot to lay your head each night.

Quality sleep will allow you to fully enjoy your daytime activities and return from your trip completely relaxed.

We will provide you with a list of highly-rated hotels and AirBnbs at different prices so you can choose the one that appeals to you most.



5-Star Food


Niki’s a major foodie and always finds the best local fare, food halls, secret eateries, and more.

We’ll ensure that you leave your destination having tried the best dishes and treats offered.

Have dietary restrictions? Let us know and we’ll research the menus or contact the restaurants on your behalf.



Unique Activities


During our free consultation with you, we’ll ask a series of questions about the type of trip you envision.

Whether you like relaxing on a beach, going on as many day trips as possible, or a combination of both, we’ll talk through how to balance the trip to include everything on your bucket list.
