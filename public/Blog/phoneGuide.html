Using Your Phone Abroad


Option 1: Is your current plan eligible for international-use?

Most providers come with an option to use your plan internationally, the best way to determine whether or not this applies to you is by visiting your online account, and seeing if there is the option to either add this on or initialize it.

If you don't have access to an online account, I would give them a call or visit a local branch, they will be able to determine quickly whether or not it is included in your current plan, and can even add it on if you'd like?

Want to avoid this all together? Check out option 2...



Option 2: Purchase an international sim card.

Go online and purchase an international sim card, we recommend this one. which is a company called '3' out of the UK, this is the sim card we use when we travel.



What do you get?

1. A trio size (nano/micro/std) SIM which will fit all phones, make sure your device is GSM unlocked either 3G or 4G compatible.
2. A Prepaid bundle of 12GB data, 3000 voice mins, 3000 texts for 30 days use.



How to activate:

1. Simply insert SIM into your unlocked phone, make sure you enable data roaming in your settings.
2. Wait for the signal, accept Three UK setting messages, then you are all set to go.
3. Test it in the US before your travel.



How to check your balance and top up?

1. Call 333 from your handset and follow the instructions.
2. Download the 'Three' app, and top up this way.

Remember, every time you top up, turn your phone off and on to activate the top-up.



What restrictions are there?

1. Use in a phone only.
2. 4G signal is available dependent on geographical locations, see Three website for further information.
3. Please note you cannot use the 3000 mins/ texts to call or text US numbers. In order to do this, you would need to add credit to the account by purchasing a top up voucher. Calls to USA costs 3p per minute.
4. Top up vouchers are available from third party online resellers, whilst Three UK only offer this, if you hold an UK credit / debit card.

Three UK customer services: From your Three phone dial 333, From abroad +44 7782 333 333
